Brand Concept: FoundCoffee

Canned coffee for college campuses.

Canned coffee sits heated and chilled in vending machines in Japan's metro. For my marketing managment class, I worked with a group of three other students to bring the same concept to our college campus. Our idea was to make this beverage "fun," "hip," and "cool."
After learning that students find their favorite brands through word-of-mouth and choose those brands (think Glossier and Supreme) because of unique designs and packaging, I focused on making our branding distinct. I went for bright colors, line drawings, and animations.

Students love customizing their laptops and waterbottles with stickers. These designs are brightly colored, but consistent.
